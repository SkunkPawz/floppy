Recent Thoughts on Things
June 6th- I honestly think the internet was better when everything was ugly like in our modern times everything has to look as polished as possible: our social media pages, our portfolios, our art, our expression. Everything goes towards building up a "personal brand", and self expression has just become something else to optimise in the name of profit. I think this corresponds with the death of the "bad artist". Creating dumb little animations and "cringe" art was so important and so healthy, and now people are encouraged to only post the best of the best, to hand polish everything, to the point where expressing yourself is more like unpaid labor than a creative outlet.
